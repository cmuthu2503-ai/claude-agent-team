## Executive Summary

As of November 2024, the large language model landscape is dominated by three major providers: Anthropic's Claude, OpenAI's GPT, and Google's Gemini. Each offers distinct advantages in terms of context handling, pricing, and capabilities.

**Key Findings:**
- **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** leads in context window size (200K tokens) and cost-effectiveness
- **GPT-4 Turbo** offers the most mature ecosystem and widespread integration
- **Gemini Pro** provides competitive pricing and strong multimodal capabilities
