# Slide 3: Market Leaders
- **Anthropic Claude**: Constitutional AI, strong reasoning
- **OpenAI GPT**: Mature ecosystem, widespread adoption
- **Google Gemini**: Massive context windows, competitive pricing

Speaker notes: The LLM market has consolidated around these three major providers, each with distinct strengths and positioning.

# Slide 4: Context Window Comparison
- **Gemini 1.5 Pro**: 2,000,000 tokens (industry leader)
- **Claude 3.x models**: 200,000 tokens
- **GPT-4 models**: 128,000 tokens

Speaker notes: Context window size directly impacts the model's ability to process large documents and maintain long conversations.

# Slide 5: Pricing Landscape
**Most Affordable:**
- Gemini Flash: $0.075/$0.30 per million tokens
- GPT-4o Mini: $0.15/$0.60 per million tokens

**Premium Tier:**
- Gemini Pro: $1.25/$5.00 per million tokens
- Claude Sonnet: $3.00/$15.00 per million tokens

Speaker notes: Pricing varies significantly based on model tier and use case requirements. Input/output pricing differs substantially.

---

# Slide 6: Performance Strengths
- **Claude**: Superior reasoning, coding, analysis
- **GPT**: Well-rounded, extensive integrations
- **Gemini**: Large document processing, multimodal

Speaker notes: Each model family has developed distinct competitive advantages based on training approach and optimization focus.

# Slide 7: Use Case Recommendations
- **Large Documents**: Choose Gemini Pro (2M context)
- **General Purpose**: Choose GPT-4o (ecosystem)
- **Cost-Sensitive**: Choose Gemini Flash (best value)
- **Complex Reasoning**: Choose Claude Sonnet (analytical strength)

Speaker notes: Organizations should match model selection to specific use case requirements rather than adopting a single-model approach.

# Slide 8: Strategic Approach
- Evaluate multi-model strategy

- Monitor market evolution

# Slide 9: Key Takeaways
- No single model dominates all use cases
- Context window and pricing are key differentiators
